THE COOK

Born in Colombo, Sri Lanka, and having grown up and travelled throughout the world, Harin developed an enthusiastic taste for diverse and eclectic flavors early.

Though he enjoys experimenting with different cuisines and dishes, the humble sausage has proven to be a perfect vehicle to carry any of his favorite ingredients and flavors, flexible and accommodating enough to carry South Asian spices, a little Southern US soul and heat, and just about anything in between.

Although an actuary by profession, there’s no exact mathematics or formula to his recipes – though he always throws in a dash of something from his favorite places in the world.

THE ASSISTANTS

Harin would not be the successful sausage chef that he is today without the support and assistance of Kay and Kalla, his esteemed sous chefs. Yes, it is true that Harin prepares each sausage to his exacting specifications himself, as no one else in the world can be trusted to season and sear them quite right, his assistants play a large role in selection and preparation of the meat, stocking of the spices and clean-up before and after the master has worked. As well, Kay and Kalla prepare complementary dishes for consumption alongside the sausage, though the sausage is of course the centrepiece of the meal.

Both Kay and Kalla continue to grow as chefs under Harin’s tutelage and supervision, and dream to one day prove themselves worthy of learning the secrets to Harin’s sausage.
